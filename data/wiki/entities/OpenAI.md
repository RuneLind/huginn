# OpenAI

AI research company, creator of GPT models and ChatGPT. Primary competitor to [[Anthropic]].

## Key Products (as of early 2026)

- **GPT-5.4** — unified flagship model merging GPT-5.2 and GPT-5.3 Codex. Achieved 95% on planning benchmarks (highest recorded), edging out Opus 4.6's 92.9%
- **SPUD** — new model focused on persistent reasoning. Sora (video generation) was killed to free compute for SPUD
- **Codex** — coding agent (CLI-based, similar to [[Claude Code]])
- **ChatGPT** — consumer AI product, leveraging brand strength for enterprise adoption

## Key People

- **Sam Altman** — CEO. Identified consumer brand strength as key enterprise advantage
- **[[Andrej Karpathy]]** — co-founder, departed (now at Eureka Labs)

## Competitive Dynamics

- Anthropic rapidly gaining enterprise customers through Claude Code and the Max subscription
- Google competing with Gemini 3/4 models
- December 2025 marked a "phase transition" — three frontier models (Claude, Gemini, GPT) launched within six days
- MCP joining the Linux Foundation's Agentic AI foundation enables interoperable tool use across providers

## Organizational Turbulence

Sources reference significant departures: almost every original OpenAI dinner attendee left after clashing with Altman — including Elon Musk (founded xAI), [[Dario Amodei]] (founded Anthropic), and others. OpenAI also reportedly slowing hiring as AI agents replace some engineering roles internally.
