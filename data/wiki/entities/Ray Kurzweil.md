# Ray Kurzweil

Futurist, inventor, and author of *The Singularity is Near* (2005). One of the most prominent voices on [[AGI and Singularity Predictions|AGI timelines]].

## Key Predictions

- The **Singularity has started** as of 2026
- Defines AGI as expert-level capability across **thousands of fields** simultaneously — stricter than most definitions
- Predicts humanity **1,000x smarter by 2045** through human-AI merging
- Advocates **longevity escape velocity** — the point where life extension technology adds more than one year of life per year

## Influence

Kurzweil's 2005 framework is widely cited by others making AGI predictions, including Elon Musk. His exponential growth models for technology have been directionally accurate over decades, though specific timelines are debated.
