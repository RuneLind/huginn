# Dario Amodei

CEO and co-founder of [[Anthropic]]. Former VP of Research at [[OpenAI]].

## AGI Predictions

- Predicts **AGI with Nobel-level intellect** arriving by **2026-2027**
- Describes AGI as capable of navigating human digital interfaces and interacting with the physical world
- Famous warning: "You have no idea what's coming in 6 months"
- More conservative than some peers (e.g., [[Ray Kurzweil]]) but still aggressive on timelines

## Pentagon Clash

In early 2026, Anthropic faced a public clash with the US Pentagon and Trump administration:

- **Defense Secretary Pete Hegseth** threatened to cancel government contracts, declare a supply chain risk, or invoke the Defense Production Act
- The dispute centered on AI usage terms for the US military
- Dario's public interview response drew significant attention
- Highlighted tensions between AI safety principles and government/military demands

> [!note]
> This event is covered from multiple angles across sources — the Pentagon interview, an autonomous weapons expert's perspective, and an AI whistleblower's analysis. The consensus is that it was a pivotal moment for AI governance.

## On Anthropic's Strategy

- Building "for the model 6 months from now, not today's model"
- Infrastructure being laid for Opus 5/6 generation
- Emphasizes AI safety alongside rapid capability development
